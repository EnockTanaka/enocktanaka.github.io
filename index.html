OUR VALUES

At Traditional Methodist Church We
Practice Grace & Holiness

Following in the tradition of John Wesley, we emphasize personal and social holiness, seeking to grow in love of God and neighbor while working for justice in our communities.

ABOUT US
TRADITIONAL METHODIST TRADITION

Our Church Embraces Wesley's Vision of
Faith Working Through Love

We are committed to the Wesleyan quadrilateral of Scripture, tradition, reason, and experience as we seek to understand God's will for our lives and our world.

Scripture

We believe the Bible is the primary source for Christian doctrine, revealing the nature of God and path to salvation.

Grace

We emphasize God's prevenient, justifying, and sanctifying grace that transforms hearts and lives.

Service

We are called to put faith into action through service to others and working for justice in our world.
